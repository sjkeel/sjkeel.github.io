How I desighned this impracticle piece of futuristic crap

The Design

I wanted the hull to by cylindrical since the modules shipped from earth would most likely be done by rocket. In the middle I designed a hypothetical fusion drive since that would be the ideal and practical power source for my ship. In the rear I have the main propulsion thrusters and right next to that I have the radar dishes that would serve no valuable purpose since they are right next to the engine. I should have had them further up the main hull and pointed forward to listen for other ships. Also I have just a bunch of antennas and sensors sticking out of this thing with no rhyme or reason. What I will do in the future.

Model specific parts for a specific purpose to ensure a look of functionality instead of random cool elements mashed together. I did well with the propulsion and fusion reactor. I would also design it to be more modular to make it seem to actually be manufactured. I also am thinking of building an orbital platform to dock it too.

Missing Elements Include:

  • Docking station
  • Wires/Pipes
  • Maybe some drone attachments
  • depth of field
  • Scale

What I learned

Have a pre specified function to the ship. Describe the purpose and available assets used to build the ship before designing. ALWAYS USE REFERENCE MATERIAL!
